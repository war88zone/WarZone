Realisations

This website made for a photographer is one of my first development. Built from scratch, I decided to design it with a special atmosphere. The glass effect represents the finess of the artist, and her ability to capture the moment, like through a window on the world.

The design has been thinking mobile first and all the resolutions are supported.

Translated in 3 languages (French, English and Russian), the website allows the artist to publish her pictures, videos, describe herself and share her social networks.
